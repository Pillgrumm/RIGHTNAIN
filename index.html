🗡️ Night Reign: Fast Route

Day 1 – Route to Level 10 & Purples

  • 🔹 Start: Clear church or encampment → blue gear, level 3.
  • 🔹 Hit another church if nearby; ignore mines.
  • 🔹 Head to castle mid-map → sewer boss (skip if bell hunter).
  • 🔹 Get talisman chest upstairs.
  • 🔹 Courtyard: Clear if not Giants-themed.
  • 🔹 Aim to hit level 10 before the night boss.

⚠️ Got bell hunter + Giants theme? Bad luck, just salvage the run.

Day 2 – Buffs & Legendary Gear

  • 🔹 Check if Shifting Earth is active.
  • Volcano: Church → lava hallway → Magma Worm → Smith → Spirit Bird → N Crater.
  • Mountain: South fight → skip frost dragon → get mountain buff → return for kill.
  • Forest: Fortress → map → buff tree → bosses → day boss.
  • Night City: Sewer → city end boss → 2nd life buff → exit center path → boss.
  • No event? Clear castle top floor & field bosses.

🎯 End Day 2 at level 13+ with legendaries and a biome buff. Nightlord becomes manageable.
